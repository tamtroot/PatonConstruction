Our Services

Professional Solutions for Every Project

At Paton Construction Ltd, we provide comprehensive groundworks and recycling solutions for domestic, commercial, and agricultural clients. Our experienced team and advanced equipment ensure efficient, eco-friendly, and cost-effective project delivery.

Onsite Crushing & Recycling

Our mobile crushing service brings efficient material processing directly to your site. Using advanced equipment, including our MB crusher bucket on Manitou telehandler, we process concrete, stone, rubble, and slabs on location.

Benefits

  • Reduce skip hire and landfill costs
  • Environmentally friendly waste reduction
  • Immediate reuse of processed materials
  • Flexible scheduling and quick turnaround
  • Suitable for both indoor and outdoor locations

Groundworks

Our comprehensive groundworks services cater to domestic, small/medium construction, and landscaping projects. With decades of combined experience, our team delivers precise and professional results for every project.

Services Include

  • Site preparation and clearance
  • Excavation and foundations
  • Drainage solutions
  • Land leveling and grading
  • Access road construction

Aggregate & Soil Processing

In collaboration with trusted partners like McLaren Plant Ltd, we provide comprehensive soil and aggregate processing services. Our expertise ensures high-quality separation and processing of various materials for immediate reuse.

Capabilities

  • Topsoil and subsoil separation
  • Aggregate processing and grading
  • Material screening and sorting
  • Custom processing solutions
  • Quality-controlled output

Agricultural Services

We extend our expertise to the agricultural sector, providing specialized equipment and services for farming operations. Our flexible booking system ensures we can accommodate your schedule and project requirements.

Available Services

  • Agricultural forklift operations
  • Material handling and transport
  • Onsite crushing services
  • Flexible scheduling options
  • Equipment rental solutions
