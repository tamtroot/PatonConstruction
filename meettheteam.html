Meet the Team

David Stewart – Construction Manager

David Stewart is the Construction Manager at Paton Construction Ltd, bringing with him a wealth of knowledge and over 35 years of groundworks management experience. Throughout his career, David has worked on a wide range of projects across Scotland and the wider UK — from small-scale developments to large, complex civil engineering works — successfully taking them from initial planning through to completion.

Specialising in civil and groundworks, David’s expertise covers every stage of a project, ensuring that work is delivered to the highest standards of quality, safety, and efficiency. He is highly regarded for his practical, hands-on approach and his commitment to supporting client requirements, making sure every project runs as smoothly as possible.

In his role, David oversees the day-to-day running of construction projects, coordinating teams, managing schedules, and resolving challenges quickly to keep work on track. He works closely alongside company owners Arron and Donna Paton, contributing to the growth and success of the family business. His dedication, leadership, and depth of industry knowledge make him an invaluable part of the Paton Construction team.
